The Problem


  • Many Americans suffer from obesity, a complex disease involving excessive body fat

  • Obesity can cause various complications such as heart disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, and even certain types of cancer

  • Every state in the U.S has at least 20% or more adults with obesity

  • Below is an interactive map displaying the percentage of adults in each state who are considered obese

Solutions


  • People can modify there diet to incorporate healthier foods consisting of fruits, vegetables, grains, proteins, and dairy

  • At least thirty minutes of exercise can help improve weight problems and create a healthier lifestyle

  • Stretching helps to improve flexibility and avoid injury when doing excercises

  • Combining strength and cardiovascular exercises in additon to a plausible diet can improve people's health, mind, and body
